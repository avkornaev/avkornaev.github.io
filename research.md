<div class="card-grid">
  <article class="info-card">
    <h3>Uncertainty-aware learning and reliable AI</h3>
    <p>This direction studies calibration, uncertainty estimation, out-of-distribution behavior, and robust evaluation for machine learning systems used in high-stakes settings.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> trustworthy prediction, distribution shift, error analysis, and evaluation protocols for real-world deployment.</p>
    <p><strong>Typical artifacts:</strong> publications, benchmarks, evaluation code, and reproducible experiments.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="info-card">
    <h3>Physics-informed neural networks and AI for scientific computing</h3>
    <p>This line of work combines machine learning with physical models, variational principles, and numerical methods for forward and inverse problems in mechanics and fluid dynamics.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> PINNs, neural surrogates, non-Newtonian flow modeling, and computational methods guided by domain knowledge.</p>
    <p><strong>Typical artifacts:</strong> research papers, simulation code, notebooks, and comparative numerical studies.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="info-card">
    <h3>AI in medicine, medical imaging, and radiogenomics</h3>
    <p>This research investigates clinically meaningful models for medical images and multimodal biomedical data, including tasks that connect imaging findings with pathology, genomics, or longitudinal clinical evidence.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> medical image analysis, multimodal fusion, radiogenomics, clinical decision support, and interpretable prediction.</p>
    <p><strong>Typical artifacts:</strong> publications, curated datasets, analysis pipelines, and model implementations.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="info-card">
    <h3>Robotics and vision-language-action systems</h3>
    <p>This direction explores how visual perception, language-conditioned reasoning, and action planning can be integrated in robotic and embodied AI systems.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> perception-action loops, multimodal grounding, planning under uncertainty, and interactive robotic systems.</p>
    <p><strong>Typical artifacts:</strong> papers, prototype code, teaching materials, and demonstration videos.</p>
  </article>
</div>
